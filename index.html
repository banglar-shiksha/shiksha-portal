About Banglar Shiksha

The Banglar Shiksha portal is an initiative by the School Education Department, Government of West Bengal, to integrate technology in the state's education system. It aims to provide a seamless interface for students, teachers, schools, and administrators to manage academic and administrative activities efficiently.
